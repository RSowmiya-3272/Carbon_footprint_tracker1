Understanding the impact of human activities on our planet.

Problem Statement

Climate change is accelerating due to excessive greenhouse gas emissions from human activities. However, most individuals and organizations are unaware of their exact environmental impact. MyCarbon addresses this gap by helping users understand and visualize their carbon footprint, empowering them to make informed decisions that reduce emissions and promote sustainability.

Definition

A Carbon Footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (GHGs) emitted directly or indirectly by human activities, products, or organizations, measured in equivalent tonnes of carbon dioxide (COâ‚‚e).

It helps assess how our energy use, travel, diet, and consumption contribute to climate change.

Main Sources of Emissions

  • Energy: Electricity generation (coal, gas) for homes and industries.
  • Transportation: Use of fossil fuels in vehicles and aircraft.
  • Food: Livestock farming and fertilizer emissions (methane and nitrous oxide).
  • Consumption: Production and disposal of manufactured goods.

Advantages of Tracking

  • Awareness of environmental impact.
  • Cost savings through efficient resource use.
  • Healthier lifestyle choices.
  • Supports climate action goals.

Challenges & Limitations

  • Complex and difficult to measure accurately.
  • Shifts focus from corporate responsibility.
  • Requires reliable data collection.
  • Often excludes government-scale emissions.
